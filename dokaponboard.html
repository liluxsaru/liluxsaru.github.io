GOLD ROAD
— BOARD RIVALS —
Roll dice and race around the loop. Gold tiles pay out or bite back. ⚔ Battle tiles pit you against a monster in a best-of-3 Rock-Paper-Scissors duel for loot. 🛒 Shops sell permanent power-ups. 🏰 Castles can be claimed — owners collect toll from anyone who lands there. ❓ Event tiles are wildcards. 🌀 Warp flings you elsewhere on the board.

Land on a rival's tile and you choose: Attack for a best-of-3 Rock-Paper-Scissors duel over gold, or Spare them for a small goodwill bonus. Win a duel 2-0 for a bonus payout. Higher ATK and DEF sweeten your rewards and soften your losses. After the set number of rounds, whoever has the highest net worth (gold + castles + power) wins.
